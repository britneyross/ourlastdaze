Forged from the burgeoning music scene of Central Texas, Our Last Daze is a dynamic and ever evolving force to be reckoned with. Powerful instrumentation shifting from heavy to poppy to soulful, accompanied by catchy vocal melodies with emotive lyrical themes of the debauchery of youth and the existential dread that follows, Our Last Daze is reminiscent of the mid-2000’s Emo/Pop-Punk scene crossbred with modern post-hardcore and a sprinkle of something else entirely. There is no better way to truly experience Our Last Daze than live. Be it a dilapidated shed, your living room, the local dive bar or a multilevel live music venue, they fill the space and somehow provide a high energy, yet intimate experience for the audience, who are guaranteed to have a truly cathartic experience.
